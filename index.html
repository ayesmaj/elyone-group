The Reality

It only takes one slip.

Most bathroom dangers are completely predictable — and preventable. Here's what makes your bathroom risky right now.

Stepping out of the shower

Wet feet on a slippery floor — the most common moment a fall happens.

Slippery floors

Tile and vinyl become like ice when wet. Even a small amount of water is enough.

Nothing to hold onto

Without grab bars, there's no way to catch yourself when balance is lost.

Low light at night

A midnight trip to the bathroom in the dark is disorienting and dangerous.

"That moment where you say: 'That was close…' — that's the warning sign. Don't wait for the next one."
